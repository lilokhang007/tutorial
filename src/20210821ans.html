Question 1

The following question concerns about the polymer PET.

  1. Draw the monomer and repeating unit of PET.

    2. Monomer

    Repeating unit

  2. Suggest one use of PET.

    3. Food packaging, bottles, etc

  3. Explain whether PET can be used to store drain cleansers.

    4. No, drain cleansers contain concentrated NaOH.

Question 2

You are provided with four unlabelled bottles each containing one of the following colourless liquids:

1-bromobutane butanoic acid cyclohexene propanone

Outline a scheme of tests to distinguish the four liquids from one another.

Add water to the liquids. Both butanoic acid and propanone can mix with water in all properties.

Add a piece of pH paper to the aqueous solutions. Solution of butanoic acid is acidic while that of propanone is not.

Add aqueous bromine to the remaining two compounds. Only cyclohexene can decolorize the aqueous bromine quickly.

Add silver nitrate solution to the remaining two compounds. Only 1-bromobutane gives a pale yellow precipitate slowly.
