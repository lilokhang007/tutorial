Answers for 2021-03-02

Fill in the blanks with the correct tenses.

  • People __________ (suffer) from chronic illnesses should pay more attention to their health.

    People suffering from chronic illnesses should pay more attention to their health.

  • They should seek medical attention if _________ (feel) unwell.

    They should seek medical attention if feeling unwell.

  • Seasonal influenza __________ (be) a common respiratory tract infection __________ (cause) by human seasonal influenza viruses.

    Seasonal influenza is a common respiratory tract infection caused by human seasonal influenza viruses.
