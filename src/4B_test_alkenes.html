Chemical test: alkenes

Notes

Bromine or potassium permaganate can be decolorized by organic compounds containing C=C double bond.

Example:

  1. ✔ Ethene
  2. ✔ Cyclohexene.
  3. ✘ Heptane. (No observable change)

The reaction is an addition reaction.
