Draw the following angles. VE is the height of the pyramid.

  1. Angle between the plane VAB and the plane ABCD.

    Let M be the midpoint of AB. ∠VME.

  2. Angle between the plane VAC and the plane ABCD.

    ∠VEB or ∠VED = 90°.

  3. Angle between the plane VAC and the plane VBC.

    Let X be a point on VC such that AX ⊥ VC and BX ⊥ VC. ∠AXB.
