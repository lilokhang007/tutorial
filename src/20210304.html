True or False: Determine whether the following statements must be true.

  1. The sum of two sides of a triangle is always larger than the third side.
  2. The sum of two angles of a triangle is always larger than the third angle.
  3. If a < 0 < b, then ab < 0.
  4. If 1/a < 1/b, then ab(a - b) > 0.

Answers for 2021-03-03

In the following figure, ΔOAB has an area of 16 unit squared.

  • Find the value of a.

    • 8*a/2 = 16

    a = 4
  • L1 is the perpendicular bisector of AB.
    • Find the equation of L1.

      • Let the midpoint of AB be M. M = (4,2)

      Slope of AB = -4/8 = -0.5

      Slope of AB * Slope of perpendicular bisector = -1

      Slope of perpendicular bisector = 2

      Let the equation be y = 2x + c

      Substiute (4,2) into the equation, we have:

      2 = 2(4) + c

      c = -6

      The equation is y = 2x - 6
    • L1 crosses the x-axis at C and the y-axis at D respectively. Find the ratio of areas ΔOCD:ΔOAD.

      • Let the coordinates of C be (p,0), we have:

      0 = 2p - 6

      p = 3

      C = (3,0)

      Ratio of areas ΔOCD:ΔOAD

      =OC : OA

      =3 : 8
