Answers for 2021-03-01

Fill in the blanks with the correct tenses.

  • Once upon a time, there was a girl who __________ (name) Cinderella.

    Once upon a time, there was a girl who was named Cinderella.

  • Cinderella __________ (bully) by her stepsisters and their mother.

    Cinderella was bullied by her stepsisters and their mother.

  • Cinderella __________ (live) very happily until her mother died.

    Cinderella had lived very happily until her mother died.
