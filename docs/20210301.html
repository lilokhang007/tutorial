2021-03-01

Fill in the blanks with the correct tenses.

  • Once upon a time, there was a girl who __________ (name) Cinderella.

  • Cinderella __________ (bully) by her stepsisters and their mother.

  • Cinderella __________ (live) very happily until her mother died.

Answers for 2021-02-28

Proofread the following sentences, there may be NO or more than 1 mistake in each sentence.

  • Once upon a time, there was a girl named Cinderella.

    Once upon a time, there was a girl named Cinderella. (Correct).

  • She lived unhappily to her stepsisters and their mother.

    She lived unhappily with her stepsisters and their mother.

  • They treated Cinderella very bad.

    They treated Cinderella very badly.
