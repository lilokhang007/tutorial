Answers for 2021-02-27

Proofread the following sentences, there may be more than 1 mistake in each sentence.

  • I was interested in Biology since Form Three.

    I have been interested in Biology since Form Three.

  • There have many benefits to study English.

    There are many benefits to study English.

  • Here have two books I buy for you.

    Here are two books I have bought for you.
